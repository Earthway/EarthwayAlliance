Our Team

Meet the dedicated and passionate team behind Earthway Alliance Company. Our experts are committed to creating sustainable solutions for environmental conservation and climate resilience.

Amos Chepkwony

Founder and CEO Earthway Alliance Company

With several years of experience in climate change and environmental restoration, Amos has successfully led impactful initiatives across Kenya. His expertise spans afforestation, reforestation, Farmer Managed Natural Regeneration (FMNR), and climate-smart agriculture. Amos has coordinated large-scale tree-growing projects, including the planting of over 500,000 trees, restoring thousands of hectares of degraded land. He has trained 1,785 households in FMNR and agroforestry practices, supported the establishment of environmental clubs in schools, and built the capacity of community groups on sustainable land management. His efforts have empowered communities in counties such as Baringo and Nakuru to adopt climate-resilient practices, improving livelihoods while enhancing carbon sequestration and biodiversity.

Dennis Kimeli

Board Member,GIS/RS/Carbon/Climate/

Dennis is a distinguished GIS specialist with over a decade of experience in ecosystem management, spatial analysis, and environmental policy development. His expertise lies in utilizing Remote Sensing and advanced geospatial technologies to support conservation planning, biodiversity monitoring, and sustainable land-use strategies. As a trusted advisor to Earthway Alliance, Dennis has been instrumental in mapping critical areas for conservancies and identifying regions requiring urgent protection. His work ensures that conservation efforts are data-driven and strategically targeted, optimizing resource allocation and enhancing the effectiveness of environmental interventions. Dennis's insights have significantly contributed to preserving vital ecosystems and promoting climate resilience in key landscapes.

Joanne Biwott

Data Manager

Joanne, with over five years of experience in data analysis, has played a pivotal role in generating actionable insights to support afforestation and reforestation projects. Her expertise in data collection, processing, and visualization has empowered communities by guiding sustainable environmental practices and enhancing project efficiency and impact.

Dennis Biegon

Lead Analyst GIS and Rmote Sensing

Dennis has over five years of experience in environmental management, specializing in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Remote Sensing technologies. His expertise includes spatial data analysis, land use mapping, and environmental monitoring to support conservation and sustainable land management initiatives. Passionate about raising awareness and driving impactful conservation efforts, Dennis uses Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification (MRV) applications to communicate data-driven insights. By leveraging advanced geospatial tools, he creates engaging stories that highlight the challenges and successes of environmental conservation, inspiring communities and stakeholders to take climate-positive actions.
